Opus

App for jobs.

View Prototype



Problem statement

How might we help daily wage workers in finding their day’s job easily? How might we help contractors in finding suitable and quality daily workers?




Primary user

Workers looking for their day job. Employer/Contractor or individuals who are looking to hire workers.




Point of view

A person(labour, construction workers, carpenter, plumber etc) who earns on a daily basis need to visit markets daily for new jobs because if he won’t work for a day, he will be in a financial crisis. A contractor(construction company or individual) travel to local markets to hire workers.







How might we

​How might we help daily workers in securing a job? How might we help the person(worker’s) in securing new jobs by sitting at home so that they don’t have to spend time and money travelling to local market in search of a job? How might we help the person in making money easily? How might we help to improve the age old method of getting and giving job to daily earning worker’s? How might we help a company or individual in hiring their workers without spending time and money on travelling to the market ?
